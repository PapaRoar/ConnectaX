🌟 Welcome to ConnectaX! 🌟
Your ultimate all-in-one platform designed to unite people and create unforgettable digital experiences.

Key Features:

  • 1️⃣ Chat & Private Messaging – Stay connected with friends or enjoy private conversations anytime. 💬
  • 2️⃣ Share Moments – Instantly share photos and videos to capture and celebrate life’s highlights. 📸
